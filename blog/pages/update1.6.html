New features in version 1.6

Changelog in Power Widgets Update 1.6.

This update adds the ability to take notes and pin them to a dedicated note view. We've also added OneNote integration so you can work on OneNote pages right from the Notes panel. You can also pin your notebooks to get quick access to certain pages.


Navigation bar

Navigation bar allows fast navigation in widgets feed. When you click on a certain item in the bar, you will be transferred to the corresponding place. The navigation bar is fully customizable to your liking. You can also attach websites to it.


Redesigned clock widget

Introducing new clock widget with today event displaying. Now you can see today's event directly on the time widget, also if you want to see more just click on the dropdown button. As an addition to this, there is a new button for a quick transition to the alarm clock and clock application. Previously, this could be done by clicking on the time, by the way, this has not gone away.



Outlook widget new features

After this update you will be can do more with your mailbox for example: from now you can delete your messages or review in the new Outlook integrated client. New mail view dialog makes your interaction with mail more conveniently and friendly.


New calendar view UI will make your work more efficiently and easier

Peoples feature is also updated in Power Widgets 1.6. Now you can manage your all contacts list and pin this to fast access or navigation bar.
